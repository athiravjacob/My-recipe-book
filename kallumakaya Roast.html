Kallumakaya Roast

Ingridients

  • 1/2 kg Mussels/kallumakkai
  • 2 Onion
  • 4 Green chilly
  • 1 large Tomato
  • 2 tsp Ginger garlic paste
  • 3 tsp Red chilly powder
  • 1 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 3/4 tsp Garam masala
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Curry leaves little

Direction

Step 1

Clean mussels/kallumakkai and marinate it in chilly powder,turmeric powder,salt.

Step 2

Heat oil in a vessel and deep fry the mussels.Heat oil in a pan and saute onion until it is golden brown in colour.

Step3

Add tomato,green chilly,ginger,garlic,curry leaves,coriander leaves and saute for 5 minutes.Add fried mussels,garam masala,salt and saute for 2 minutes.
